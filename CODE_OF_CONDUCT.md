# Code of Conduct

PyTransformer should be a useful, respectful place for engineers to collaborate.

## Standards

Expected behavior:

- Be clear, constructive, and patient when discussing code or user workflows.
- Keep feedback focused on behavior, correctness, maintainability, and documentation.
- Respect privacy. Do not post files, metadata, transcripts, logs, or local paths that expose private information.
- Assume good intent, and make room for people to ask basic questions.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing another person's private information.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions after maintainers ask to refocus.
- Using issues, pull requests, or discussions to pressure maintainers for unrelated support.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to Paul Tocatlian through <https://www.tocatlian.com>.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, restrict participation, or take other reasonable steps to keep the project professional and useful.
